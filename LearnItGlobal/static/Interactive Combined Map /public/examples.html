🚇 Interactive Subway Maps Demo

How to Use in Django

To integrate these maps into your Django website, simply add a div with the data-subway-map attribute:

<div data-subway-map="purple"></div>
<div data-subway-map="red"></div>
<div data-subway-map="green"></div>
<div data-subway-map="blue"></div>

Available maps: purple, red, green, blue
